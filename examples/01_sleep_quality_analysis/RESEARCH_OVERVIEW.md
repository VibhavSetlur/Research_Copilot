# Research Overview: Sleep Quality Intervention

## Context
This project investigates the efficacy of a new behavioral intervention aimed at improving sleep quality among adults. We have collected a small sample dataset containing baseline sleep scores and post-intervention sleep scores for both a control group and a treatment group.

## Research Intent
"Explore whether the intervention significantly improves sleep scores compared to the control group, and summarize the key statistical findings (e.g., Welch's t-test, Cohen's d)."

## Data Dictionary
- `participant_id`: Unique identifier for each subject.
- `age`: Age of the participant.
- `group`: Assignment group (`control` or `treatment`).
- `baseline_sleep_score`: Sleep quality score prior to the intervention (0-100).
- `sleep_score`: Sleep quality score following the intervention period (0-100).
